Privacy notice

What we collect, and why

If you use the Register interest form, we collect the name, email address, and any message you give us. We use these only to add you to a small private interest list and to contact you, in time, should the house become available. The legal basis is your consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)), given when you submit the form — your registration is complete on submission, and you may withdraw at any time.

How the form is delivered

Submissions are handled by Brevo (Brevo SAS, France, formerly Sendinblue), acting as our processor — it stores the interest list. Brevo processes this data within the European Union.

Hosting

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., a Microsoft company) and delivered through Cloudflare (Cloudflare, Inc.), whose network routes and protects the traffic. Like any web host, both process limited technical data such as your IP address in server logs, to deliver and protect the site (legitimate interest, GDPR Article 6(1)(f)). This may involve processing outside the EU under appropriate safeguards (such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses and the EU–US Data Privacy Framework).

No tracking, no third-party fonts

The site self-hosts its fonts and uses no analytics, advertising, or social-media trackers. Opening a page therefore sends your data to no third party beyond the hosting providers above.

How long we keep it

We keep your details on the interest list until you ask us to remove you, or until the list is no longer maintained.

Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your data, or object to its use, and you may withdraw consent at any time — simply write to aethon-house-cy@outlook.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Office of the Commissioner for Personal Data Protection (Cyprus). We make no automated decisions and do not profile you.

Cookies & local storage

This site sets no advertising or analytics cookies. It stores a single functional preference — your choice of day or night view — in your browser's local storage, and only when you use the toggle. It is never sent to us, and you can clear it through your browser at any time. Because there is no non-essential tracking, no cookie-consent banner is needed.
